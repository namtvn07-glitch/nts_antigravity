# ASO Design Plan: Yoya Sparkle (Semi-Realistic)

## 1. Project Information
- **Project Folder:** `YoyaSparkle_ASO`
- **Style Profile:** Semi-Realistic
- **Art Style / Vibe:** Glamorous, trendy, clean illustration, high-end modern anime background aesthetics. Clean, serene, beautifully illuminated.
- **Anchor Character (Sử dụng chung cho tất cả Screenshots):** A beautiful teenage fashionista with flowing platinum blonde hair in soft waves, glowing fair skin, and expressive pastel blue eyes.
- **LƯU Ý QUAN TRỌNG:** Sẽ chạy công cụ `generate_image`. KHÔNG TẠO CHỮ (No typography/text) trên bất kỳ bức ảnh nào. Ảnh phải là minh hoạ sạch thuần tuý.

## 2. Style Modifier (Áp dụng cho mọi Prompt)
"In the style of high-quality premium anime background art and modern visual novels, featuring clean, subtle edge definition without heavy black outlines. Use a harmonious pastel color palette with warm, glowing ambient lighting, luminous tinted shadows, and inviting contrast. Employ soft, painterly shading with crisp highlights to render smooth, idealized materials lacking detailed grit or noise. The overall atmosphere should be pristine, serene, and beautifully illuminated, capturing an idealized yet grounded realistic environment. No text, no typography."
